Client: UNL LGBTQA+ Resource Center

Application: Social Activism Campaign

The UNL LGBTQA+ Resource Center is located in the Student Union at the University of Nebraska Lincoln campus. They service their students, helping with bullying, academics, among other things. They were looking for a social activism campaign that would help spread the word about the misconceptions about the LGBT community along with labeling. Targetting students at the college who are fresh out of highschool or have been attending classes for a few years.
