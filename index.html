Who is

Chazgazm or her past name, Chasity Vodehnal, is a graphic, and I mean GRAPHIC, designer who believes in pushing the norms and penetrating the status quo. She graduated from the Graphic Design | Media Arts program at the Lincoln, Nebraska Southeast Community College. She is passionate about expressing her sexuality and love of illustration. She works best in small teams (or maybe a threesome) and enjoys making visuals to stand out amongst the mundane and “play-it-safe” designers.
